2️⃣ PhotoVoltaic System

kW
Total solar panel capacity. Typical residential: 3-10 kW, Commercial: 50-500 kW
hrs
Peak Sun Hours (PSH) = equivalent hours of full sun (1,000 W/m²) per day.

🇵🇭 Philippine average: 4.0–4.5 hrs/day (conservative baseline).
Raw geographic potential: 4.5–5.5 hrs/day.

Using 4.0 hrs accounts for clouds, monsoon rain, panel losses, and degradation.

Use the Calculate button above to estimate PSH by region, orientation, and tilt.
